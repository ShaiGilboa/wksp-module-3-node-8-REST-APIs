The hangman game!

How to play?

Once you click on the 'start' button you will see the lines that represent the amount of letters you will need to find.

On the right corner you will see the amount of guesses you have left, and on the left you will see the letters that you have guessed

In order to make your guess, just press the letter key on your keyboard

If you choose the have a hint, it will cost you 1 attempt

press 'space' key to continue
